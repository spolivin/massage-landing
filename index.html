Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your first session.

Swedish Massage uses long, flowing strokes to promote full-body relaxation and improve circulation — ideal if you're new to massage or looking to unwind. Deep Tissue targets the deeper layers of muscle and connective tissue using slower, firmer pressure, making it better suited for chronic tension, stiffness, or specific problem areas. If you're unsure, our therapists will help you choose during your consultation.

Arrive a few minutes early to complete a short health questionnaire. Your therapist will walk you through the treatment plan, answer any questions, and ensure you're comfortable throughout. After your session you'll have time to rest before leaving.

For general relaxation and stress relief, once or twice a month is ideal. If you're addressing a specific issue such as chronic pain or sports recovery, weekly sessions may be recommended initially. Your therapist will advise based on your individual needs.

Hot Stone massage is deeply relaxing but not suitable for everyone. We advise against it if you have cardiovascular conditions, diabetes, skin sensitivities, or are pregnant. Please let us know about any health conditions when booking and our therapists will confirm whether it is appropriate for you or suggest an alternative.

We ask for at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a cancellation fee of 50% of the session price. We understand that life happens — please contact us as early as possible if your plans change.
